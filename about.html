Our Story

Built on Trust, Rooted in Community

Argy Plumbing and Heating Ltd. is a women-owned plumbing, heating, and gas fitting company based right here in Calgary's Citadel community. We were founded on a simple belief: homeowners deserve honest quotes, professional workmanship, and a plumber who actually shows up on time.

Sam — the master plumber behind every job — has spent years building a reputation as someone who goes above and beyond. Whether it's lending tools to a neighbour fixing their travel trailer, staying late to get a furnace working during a cold snap, or walking a homeowner through a repair step-by-step, he treats every call like it's his own home.

We don't believe in overcharging, overselling, or wasting your time. We come prepared, we fix it right the first time, and we leave you with clear answers and fair pricing.
